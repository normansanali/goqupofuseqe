    It's the small things! Travis Barker is one happy guy. The Blink-182 drummer gushed about Rita Ora just days before Us Weekly exclusively confirmed that they are dating.

    Barker, 39, posted a sweet Instagram photo of the couple while attending the Power 106 All-Star Game in L.A. on Sept. 20. "Me and the beautiful @ritaora at @power_106 #Power106AllStars," he captioned the black-and-white snapshot.

    Ora, 24, shared the pic and added: "Nice to see you too."

    As previously reported, the Fifty Shades of Grey actress — who's dated Calvin Harris and Rob Kardashian — reached out to Barker first. "They have been together every day since," a source told Us. "He’s really into her. He likes interesting women and she's beautiful."
