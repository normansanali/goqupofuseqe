    Emily Ratajkowski was all effortless elegance while out for a walk in New York City this week.

    The model traded her usual athleisure for an elevated spring/summer street look, which involved a cream-colored bustier and a long, silky skirt in the same hue by Lilysilk. She put an edgy spin on the romantic ensemble by pairing the set with a pair of black knee-high cowgirl boots from Mango, a black leather shoulder bag, and dark oval sunglasses.

    EmRata wore her hair down and opted for natural makeup and minimal jewelry—accessorizing only with a chunky gold ring and small gold hoop earrings. She had one headphone on while strolling through TriBeCa yesterday.

    TheImageDirect.com

    The My Body author has been embracing the cowgirl aesthetic as of late. Last week, she styled cowgirl boots three different ways.

    Over the weekend, she was seen walking son Sylvester Apollo Bear in his stroller while wearing black heeled cowgirl boots with white designs by Sonora, paired with an oversized plain brown T-shirt by Thirty Years, which she rolled up at the sleeves, and teeny white bike shorts.

    And the weekend prior, while spending quality time with her baby on a farm, Ratajkowski posed on the passenger seat of a car in a lavender halter top, loose high-waisted jeans, and seemingly the same pair of black Mango cowgirl boots.

    In a recent interview with POPSUGAR, EmRata admitted that her style has gotten more relaxed since she became a mother, and these days, a pair of flared leggings is one of her go-to staples.
