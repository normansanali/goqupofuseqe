    Winnie Harlow Wore a Sheer Cowl-Neck Dress to The White Party

    While the White Party draws in celebrities from every industry, it also calls on them to interpret the monochromatic dress code its named after. This came easy for model Winnie Harlow, who showed up to the annual bash wearing a stunning beaded gown.

    20 Sep 2024, 00:00

    While the White Party draws in celebrities from every industry, it also calls on them to interpret the monochromatic dress code it’s named after. This came easy for model Winnie Harlow, who showed up to the annual bash wearing a stunning beaded gown.

    The floor-length dress, from Bronx and Banco, featured a plunging cowl neckline that extended down Harlow's torso and draped over her stomach. Made out of a beaded material, the sheer ensemble also dipped in the back, revealing the model’s crystal thong. Extra material at the top of the dress turned into a hood, which Harlow wore over her long hair in some of her Instagram photos.

    She paired the delicate gown with a handful of large diamond rings, as well as some chunky clear bangle bracelets that looked like literal ice wrapped around her wrist. Nodding to the summer holiday that the party celebrated, she carried a circular shell-shaped bag, which added an opalescent sheen to her look. White, open-toed heels finished off her monochromatic look.

    With her dress doing all the talking, Harlow opted for a minimal makeup look. She accentuated her brown eyes with a subtle smokey cat-eye and wispy lashes, and wore a dewy base to keep her glowing in the Hamptons sun. A clear lip gloss, contoured by a dark brown lip liner, defined her famous pout.
