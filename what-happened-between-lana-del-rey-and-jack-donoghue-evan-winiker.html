This was quite a shock to fans, as the pair had kept their relationship low-key and fans were unaware she had a new man.

    Many people were unaware that Lana had split from Jack Donoghue, who she was linked to last summer. Del Rey never speaks about her romance, so this new update was a huge surprise.

    In early 2022, rumors began swirling that Del Rey was dating musician Jack Donoghue from the band Salem after he shared a now-deleted Instagram video of the pair at a monster truck race. In the video, Dey Rey referred to Donoghue as "babe." After the summer, the pair were not seen together.

    So, what happened between Lana Del Rey and Jack Donoghue?

    8 Who Is Jack Donoghue?

    Lana Del Rey's ex Jack Donoghue is a musician who is in the band Salem with John Holland called Salem.

    They released a 2010 critically received debut studio album titled King Night. The duo then disappeared for a decade before resurfacing in 2020 to drop Fires in Heaven.

    Jack is also involved in other artists’ projects. He has production credits on Kanye West’s Yeezus album and created a Salem remix for Charli XCX.

    7 Lana Del Rey And Jack Donoghue Appeared In Viral Images

    In July 2022, Lana Del Rey and Jack Donoghue went viral after they hard-launched their relationship on Instagram with a pair of photos taken outside of Chicago’s Cook County Jail. Although the photo has soon deleted, the post was initially captioned “Family visit.”

    Fans went wild over the photo, with many declaring it "aesthetics". There was even a viral tweet comparing the duo to Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson.

    The pair were then spotted together in May at Donoghue's birthday party, though neither party shared photos themselves.

    2 Lana Del Rey's Private Dating History

    While she rarely speaks publicly about her love life, Lana Del Rey had previously been linked to rapper G-Eazy, Italian photographer Francesco Carrozzini and Scottish singer Barrie-James O'Neill in the past.

    Del Rey was previously engaged to musician Clayton Johnson and dated Sean "Sticks" Larkin, the 46-year-old police officer, in 2020.

    It’s unclear how long Del Rey and her fiancé Evan Winiker have been dating. The couple has been spotted together in public a couple of times over the past few months, including at the Malibu Chili Cook-Off festival back in September..

    1 Why Is Lana Del Rey So Private?

    Lana Del Rey, real name Elizabeth Grant, is notoriously mysterious. She enjoys letting her music and lyrics do all the talking.

    In a black-and-white video posted on Instagram in 2021, the musician thanked fans for their support and announced her decision to delete her social media accounts.

    "I just wanted to let you know that tomorrow we are going to be deactivating my social media accounts, and that is simply because I have so many other interests and other jobs I'm doing that require privacy and transparency," Del Rey said.

    "I'm still very present and love what I do. I'm absolutely here for the music. I'm also just going on some different endeavors and I want to say thank you so much for all the support and I do hope that you like the record," she added, referencing her eighth album "Blue Banisters," which was released later that month.
