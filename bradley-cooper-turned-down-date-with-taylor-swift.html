    Taylor Swift's love life is all over the headlines these days after she broke up with Joe Alwyn, following a six-year relationship. However, a decade ago, it appears as though things were quite different for Taylor Swift, and the same holds true for Bradley Cooper.

    At the time, Cooper was enjoying a huge career surge, in large part thanks to his chemistry with Jennifer Lawrence. As we'll reveal in the following, there wasn't anything romantic between the two and in fact, Lawrence tried to set Cooper up with Taylor Swift, according to the rumor mill.

    We'll take a look at exactly what went down between Swift and Cooper, and why the date never took place. We're also going to dive deeper into Cooper relationship with Lawrence, and how fatherhood completely changed his approach to life. Cooper would also answer Howard Stern's question if there was ever anything going on between himself and Swift after Howard made remarks that they would make beautiful looking children.

    Bradley Cooper Revealed His Relationship With Jennifer Lawrence Was Strictly Professional

    Bradley Cooper and Jennifer Lawrence shared great chemistry on-screen. Fans wondered if there was anything else to their relationship, given that they were frequently cast in roles together. Cooper was quick to turn that down, making mention that their relationship wasn't like that.

    “It’s due to David O. Russell, that’s what’s happening,” Cooper said of working with Lawrence. “We did Silver Linings Playbook together. We got along great.”

    “It just didn’t happen,” Cooper said about a possible romance with his co-star. “It’s not the way we are together.”

    Cooper would go on to reveal that he got in trouble for lying about the reason they would never date. “I said that once on a red carpet just as a diffuse thing,” Cooper recalled. “And then it became a thing because the girl I was seeing [Suki Waterhouse, 23] was as young as she was.”

    The two didn't date and instead, a rumor seems to indicate that Lawrence tried to play the role of matchmaker instead. However, it didn't go as planned.

    Jennifer Lawrence Tried To Set Bradley Cooper Up With Taylor Swift, But The Actor Declined

    According to a close source with Radar Online, Bradley Cooper and Taylor Swift came very close to dating back in 2013. However, it seems like Cooper had different plans at the time, and politely declined the offer.

    The source revealed, "First of all, her reputation precedes her. Bradley is very wary of dating someone who is a bit of a serial dater like Taylor."

    "He thinks she's far too young for him and wants to date someone his own age, not 16-years younger because he's ready for something serious. It was a little awkward for Bradley. Jennifer came to him and told him that Taylor was interested in getting together. He had to politely decline, knowing full well that Jennifer and Taylor are friends. It was an ambitious move from Taylor, but she's going to have fixate on someone else as Bradley's just not that into her."

    Two years later, Cooper was asked by Howard Stern about the rumor and the actor was quick to turn it down, despite the fact that Stern suggested that the two would have great looking babies.

    Cooper said, "It's complete horse-s---," he told the show's host, Howard Stern. "I never got a call. Nothing."

    "I'm sorry my father won't be around to see his grandchildren, but he knows fatherhood was something I was looking forward to," he shared. "That's my next big step in life."

    Turns out that Bradley was speaking facts as his goal to start a family happened, and it changed his life.

    Bradley Cooper Wanted Something Serious At The Time And Was Seeking Fatherhood

    Bradley Cooper's vision was realized after he had his daughter, Lea De Seine Shayk Cooper. The experience was impactful for the actor, and it completely changed his life.

    "Fatherhood is … everything changed," Cooper said. "Every single thing is absolutely shaded by, or brought into glorious colors, by the fact that I get to be a father to a wonderful human being."

    "You have this wonderful thing or breakthrough with a script, or you have a wonderful moment on this set or in an editing room … you have like 40 of those moments every day with your kid, that are that level of joy. That's not spinning it, that's just the truth."

    Things would completely change for Swift and Cooper in the recent years. Cooper separated from Irina Shayk, while Taylor Swift made the headlines for her split from Joe Alwyn.
