    Manny Humlie and wife Audrey Humlie are now separated. Manny Humlie is currently dating girlfriend Briauna Nicol.

    Humlie is an American musical artist best known for being a member of the sibling trio band named We Three.

    We Three has released a variety of albums, such as Dear Paranoia, Sincerely Me, We’re All Messed Up -but It’s Ok, We Three, and Crackerbarrel Music.

    He is also a guitarist and pianist. The artist learned to play guitar at an early age through his dad.

    Fans may also remember him from his appearance on America’s Got Talent Season 13 alongside his band. The trio was eliminated in the Judges’ Choice in the Semifinals.

    After the show, the band continued embracing their musical career and released their self-titled debut album in December 2018.

    The album includes all of their songs from their America’s Got Talent appearances and nine new tracks. This perhaps kickstarted the band’s off-show career, bagged with successful singles.

    Manny Humlie Was Married To Audrey Humlie

    Manny Humlie and ex wife Audrey Humlie tied the knot in August 2017. The pair split after a few years of marriage, reportedly in 2020.

    The guitarist was nudged into an early marriage when people of his age had their first dates, leading to an equally early divorce.

    He was just 21 when he married Audrey.

    The former couple married in an intimate ceremony before close friends and family.

    The bride had her wedding gown fitted by ReThread Alterations, a business that makes your wedding dress and attire fit you.

    ReThread Alterations also congratulated the pair on their wedding by sharing several beautiful wedding photographs of them on their Facebook account on November 15, 2017.

    Official wedding day photos were taken by Glo Photography and the bride wore a gown from Rosewood Bridal. She had some alterations to her dress -straps shortened, under bustle installed, sides taken, and bottom 4 layers hammed.

    However, their relationship did not work for long, and soon they began facing troubles in their married life, which ultimately led to an early divorce.

    During an interview in 2021, the artist said he wrote “Half Hearted,” a track from We Three’s 2020 album “Dear Paranoia, Sincerely, Me” as he was going through a divorce from Audrey.

    Manny Humlie Current Girlfriend Briauna Nicol

    Manny Humlie and girlfriend Briauna Nicol (Rohde) have been together for over a year. Briauna is an American hairstylist and Esthetician based in Oregon.

    She graduated from Johnny Matthews’s Hairdressing Training School in May 2016. After graduating, she joined the June class at the Tangled Ends Hair Academy for Esthetics in Keizer, Oregon.

    The academy offers Esthetics in the Willamette Valley. It is a premier institute for students who desire to attain a high-quality competency-based in Barbering, Esthetics, and Hair Design.

    The institute is perfect for individuals like Nicol who is focused, motivated, and eager to finish her program at a faster pace.

    Nicol often posts her work on her Facebook account, “Briauna Nicol Oregon Stylist & Esthetician.” She also has her own personal Facebook account under the username @briauna.rohde.

    On August 27, 2022, she took to her Facebook account to give a little shout-out to her boyfriend and his band. She shared some pictures of the bandmates and wrote, “everyone come support at the portland show.”

    The band was having a Happy Tour in Portland and Seattle.

    Nicol is also an active TikTok user. She is found under the nametag @briaunanicol under the platform. She usually posts funny posts and has garnered 308 followers and 1.6k likes.

    He Has Two Siblings

    Humlie was born and raised in McMinnville, Oregon, alongside his siblings, Bethany and Joshua. The siblings make the singing and songwriting trio We Three.

    Their beloved mother taught them to breathe while their father taught them to play instruments.

    Sadly their mom passed away from cancer in 2016. They even performed an original song titled “Heaven’s Not Too Far Away” as a tribute to their mother on America’s Got Talent.

    Brother Joshua Humlie

    Drummer and pianist Joshua is the eldest of the Humlie siblings. He was born on January 27, 1991, and is 32 years old.

    America’s Got Talent’s famed musical artist Joshua is best known for being a member of We Three. Moreover, he is the owner of the Humlie School of Music in their hometown, McMinnville.

    He has been happily married to his loving wife, Kenna Humlie, since August 2014. He celebrated his 5th anniversary on August 23, 2019, by sharing several pictures of himself with his wife on Instagram.

    Joshua captioned the post, “Celebrating 5 years this month…… Go Team! Loving life with this girl. She makes me a better man and I’m still head over heels for her.”

    Kenna is also active on Instagram, where she regularly shares pictures of herself with her hubby.

    Sister Bethany Humlie

    We Three’s bassist Bethany is the middle child born to George and Kelley Humlie on April 12, 1993. She is 29 years old.

    Although her primary instrument is bass, she also plays piano. Additionally, the musical artist has been a vocal instructor for over a decade.

    The bassist launched a Kickstarted campaign with her siblings in 2013 to help raise needed funds to record a CD. She and her siblings got a standing ovation for their performance in the Season 13 premiere of AGT.

    Bethany wed Luke Blanchard in 2013. She is easily accessible on Instagram under the username @imbethanyray. With 34 posts, the beauty enjoys a fanbase of over 38.3k followers.
