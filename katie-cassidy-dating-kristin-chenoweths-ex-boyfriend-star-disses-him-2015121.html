    Meow! Katie Cassidy is dating Kristin Chenoweth’s ex-boyfriend Dana Brunetti, sources previously confirmed to Us Weekly. But it seems the Broadway vet isn’t over the breakup.

    After the new couple, who began posting Instagrams with one another following Brunetti and Chenoweth’s November 2014 split, attended the 2015 Golden Globes together on Sunday, Jan. 11, Chenoweth took to Twitter to congratulate Showtime’s The Affair on their big win — in the process dissing Brunetti’s show House of Cards, which lost out on the award.

    “Ha @KChenoweth: So happy The Affair won tonight. #deserving love Ruth Wilson xo,” Brunetti retweeted, pointing out his ex’s diss after the Netflix original series he produces lost the win for Best Drama.

    Bitterness aside, the new couple looked dashing together. The 28-year-old Arrow star stunned in a black and silver Mikael D gown, while her beau, 41, opted for a traditional black tux.

    Before going public, Cassidy addressed the rumors she was dating Brunetti on Instagram. “Dude! @dana_brunetti Apparently we give people things to talk about,” she wrote on a selfie of them on Nov. 30, just days after Us Weekly confirmed the Fifty Shades of Grey producer had split from Chenoweth, 46.

    “It was just the distance,” a source close to the former couple told Us at the time. “Dana’s career is jetting off right now, and Kristin travelled a lot already, and they just barely had time to see each other. He’s producing House Of Cards now, speaking at conferences, and has to see his kid as well. Just didn’t make sense for either party to continue.”
