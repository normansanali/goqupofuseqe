    EMILY Hampshire is an actress who is best known as Stevie Budd in the CBC comedy series Schitt's Creek.

    She has been in the entertainment industry since the 1990s.

    Who is Emily Hampshire?

    Emily Hampshire is a Canadian actress.

    Aside from Stevie in Schitt's Creek, her other best-known roles include Angelina in the 1998 romantic comedy Boy Meets Girl, Vivienne in the 2006 film Snow Cake, and Jennifer Goines in the Syfy drama series 12 Monkeys.

    In 2006, Hampshire married Matthew Smith, a former soccer player turned agent-in-training at the William Morris talent agency.

    Hampshire and Smith divorced shortly before she was cast in Schitt's Creek in 2014.

    More on Schitt's Creek cast

    In 2018, she started dating singer-songwriter Teddy Geiger.

    They got engaged that same year but parted ways in June 2019.

    How old is Emily Hampshire?

    Born on August 29, 1981, Emily Hampshire is 42 years old.

    She was born and raised in Montreal, Quebec, Canada.

    Most read in Celebrity

    The actress moved to the US in 2007 and became a naturalized US citizen in 2014.

    She is currently splitting her time between Los Angeles and Toronto.

    When was Emily Hampshire on Schitt's Creek?

    Emily Hampshire portrayed the role of Stevie Budd on Schitt's Creek from 2014 to 2020.

    Her performance earned her a Canadian Screen Award six separate times.

    In an interview with PureWow, the actress shared that the worst part of ending the show was saying goodbye to her co-stars.

    “I mean working with that cast was the best. We all are genuine friends,” she shared.

    “A lot of the time you do a series for six years with the same people, you don’t always love each other. But these were like my forever friends. They are like family really. And we miss each other I think that's the hardest thing.

    "When we ended we were saying goodbye to not only the characters but to our relationship with each other."

    Hampshire has pursued other projects after wrapping up the show.

    She's also part of the main cast of shows like The Rig and Chapelwaite.
