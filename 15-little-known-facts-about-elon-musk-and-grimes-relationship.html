    Elon Musk and Grimes are both inventive people with shared interests. Grimes used to study neuroscience, so she's a good match for Musk, who uses his rare and special brainpower to design electric cars and spaceships. He has also warned humankind about the dangers of artificial intelligence.

    These two 'spacey" celebs are both drawn to science, and they're also intensely creative. Elon actually recorded music recently, so check out his tune, Don't Doubt uR Vibe, if you want to hear something new, from an unexpected musician.

    What's most interesting about this pair is that they're reportedly going to be parents. Neither of them has come out and said that they're having a baby with the other person, but fans are definitely able to put two and two together.

    Elon and Grimes have been close for a while. Their shared interests and eccentricities keep them going strong, although it hasn't always been smooth sailing.

    15 She Is 7 Months Pregnant

    Grimes is having a baby. In fact, she's pretty far along in her pregnancy. As of early March 2020, she's at the 7-month mark. She's a bit worried about being a good mom in the future, but that's normal. We are sure this fascinating lady will rock motherhood, just like she rules pop.

    14 Elon's Been Cryptic About Grimes' Pregnancy

    Grimes has been discussing her pregnancy a lot. She's not keeping it a secret, although she doesn't talk about whether Elon contributed to her growing baby bump. Still, it's a pretty safe bet that Elon is the father of her unborn child. He posted a cryptic tweet, "x is y", and fans believe that he's referring to the baby.

    Grimes has been sleepy lately. She wants to sleep, wake up, and then sleep again. She's also been dealing with rosacea, according to Pagesix.com. This skin condition has apparently been triggered by her pregnancy.

    Grimes' openness about her pregnancy is endearing. It makes her more relatable, although she's hardly the girl next door, and we don't want her to ever be that. We doubt Elon does, either.

    13 Social Media Users Are Loving The Pregnancy

    If you love reading what people have to say on Twitter, you'll enjoy all of the commentary related to Grimes, Elon Musk, and the unborn baby.     Other posts talk about an eventual battle for world domination, between Grimes' and Elon's baby and Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner's baby.

    Grimes has an edgy image, but she can be very down to earth. She doesn't hesitate to share her thoughts about her pregnancy with fans. She's actually quite jolly and sweet when she does video chats online and stuff like that.

    Grimes is more than her sci-fi image. She's a 31-year-old woman who is full of life. She says she's going to let her baby decide on its identity, free of gender stereotypes and roles, so she's going to be an ultra-modern mother.

We think Grimes is awesome on her own, whether she has a partner or not. But the whole situation with Amber and Elon, plus the lawsuit between Johnny and Amber, may stress her out occasionally.

It's a rite of passage that a lot of women go through, and it passes. Soon enough, Grimes will have a beautiful baby to love. She has so much charm and intelligence to share with a child.
