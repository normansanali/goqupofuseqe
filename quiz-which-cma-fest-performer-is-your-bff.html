    The 2018 CMA Music Festival is hitting Music City for another year in a few short weeks, and we can’t wait to see all of the performances!

    From June 7-10, Nashville will be packed with thousands of country music fans from around the world to see their favorite artists at CMA Fest.

    The annual festival hosts hundreds of artists over four days on stages from Broadway to Nissan Stadium. With so many sets to see in such little time, CS Country curated a fun quiz to see which headliner you’d enjoy the most — and ultimately be BFFs with in your dream squad.
