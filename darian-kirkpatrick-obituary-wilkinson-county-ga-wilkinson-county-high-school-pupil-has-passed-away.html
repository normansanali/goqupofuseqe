    Darian Kirkpatrick Obituary, Death  – We are obligated to notify you of the unexpected passing of one of our pupils, Darian “Turtle” Kirkpatrick, and we do so with the deepest level of regret. Due to the fact that Turtle was such an active member of our community, particularly in the Drama Club, those who had the pleasure of knowing her will grievously miss her.

    All of those individuals are going to miss her tremendously once she leaves. Her kind and compassionate demeanor had a profound effect on the lives of a great number of individuals, and she left behind a legacy at our institution that will never be forgotten. During this trying time, we would like to offer our sincerest condolences to Turtle’s family and friends and ask that everyone keep them in their thoughts and prayers. We know that this is a tough time for everyone involved.

    Everyone in the Warrior community is upset by their loss, and we recognize that it is something that has an effect on each and every one of us individually. In the event that you want more assistance, please do not be afraid to get in touch with a member of our school’s administration or a guidance counselor at your earliest convenience.

    We are devoted to being of assistance and delivering comfort in each and every way that we can. When the time is right, we will provide any further information that is necessary on the ending arrangements. Let us come together to console one another and pay our respects to Turtle as we go on at this difficult time. Let us band together in order to do this.
