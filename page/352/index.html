Stanley Dural Jr., Buckwheat Zydeco Front Man and Accordionist, Dead at 68

03 Jul 2024, 00:00

Stanley Dural Jr., known by his stage name Buckwheat, died on Saturday in his hometown of Lafayette, Louisiana. He was 68. Since 1979, his band, Buckwheat Zydeco, brought zydeco — a style of music that combines traditional Creole instruments and sounds with R&B and rock  — to the world outside the Gulf Coast region. The popularity of the band’s live concerts led to Island Records signing Buckwheat Zydeco in 1987, making the group the first zydeco act to release an LP with a major record label.
