    Ever since debuting a pixie in spring of 2010, Michelle Williams hasn't looked back at her Dawson's-era hairstyles—until now! The star, who is one-third of our March cover, revealed that growing out her hair is currently at the top of her to-do list. "I think I'm ready! It's on its way, slowly but surely," Williams told E! News, adding that the in-between phase was the only thing stopping her. "That's why I've stayed with short hair for so long. That mid-stage is insufferable, it's really hard on your self-esteem." While we've never seen Williams's strands looking anything less than flawless, we already know what every stage of her hair transformation will look like thanks to our Hollywood Makeover Tool.
