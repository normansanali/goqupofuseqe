    Overall Meaning

    The lyrics to Fbg Wooski's song "Computers" focus on gang rivalries and street violence. The song is filled with controversial statements and references to violent acts, showcasing a sense of aggression and defiance. The lyrics mention various gangs, such as ShytSplackGang, Tooka Gang, and Tutu Gang, asserting their dominance and opposition to other rivals. Wooski boasts about his affiliations with BDK (Black Disciples Killer), CPDK (Chicago Police Department Killer), and TYMBK (Tooka Gang Killer), making it clear that he aligns himself with a violent lifestyle.


    The lyrics also contain derogatory language towards rival gangs and individuals, emphasizing a disregard for human life. Wooski describes violent acts and threats against specific people, further perpetuating the cycle of violence and retribution. The references to gang affiliations, shootings, and specific incidents are meant to assert dominance and instill fear in his enemies.


    Overall, "Computers" depicts the artist's involvement in gang culture and his commitment to retaliation against rival gangs. The lyrics serve as a form of self-expression, highlighting the harsh realities of street life and the dangers embedded within gang rivalries.
