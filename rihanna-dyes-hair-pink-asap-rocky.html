    03 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Rihanna switches up her look almost as frequently as fans ask for new music—and that’s saying something. Last week, the artist showed off her honey-blonde hair at a Fenty Beauty event in Los Angeles, and now she’s in Miami with beau A$AP Rocky and electric pink hair. (See the photos here.)

    There to support Rocky’s new Puma collaboration—released during a whirlwind weekend of F1 races—RiRi lived up to her reputation as a fashion killa. Donning a slinky black silk dress cinched with a matching lace-up corset, the singer looked impeccably cool, even while posing among multiple race cars. Her striking dye job, however, was the real star of the ensemble. Its muted neon pink color made it impossible to miss, especially while styled in long, luscious layers.

    BFA

    The dramatic beauty transformation is nothing new for Rihanna, but its debut comes just two days ahead of the Met Gala, where she will hopefully make an appearance on the famous museum steps. Guests will be dressing to the theme of “The Garden of Time,” and in this case, RiRi’s garden is looking pretty vibrant.

    Related Stories

    Of course, the singer’s beauty switch-up could be just for fun. During an interview with Extra earlier this month, the multi-hyphenate told the outlet she wanted to wear something “simple” for the gala. “I'm actually keeping it real simple this year,” she said. “It's gonna come to what my makeup and what my hair is going to do.” Whatever she wears, Rihanna is sure to turn heads—especially if she elects to keep her vivid hair color.
