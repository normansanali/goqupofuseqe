    Paulo Costa can fight, but this year, he has shown he can troll as well, and that too brutally. Costa is the former UFC middleweight title challenger and is known for his wins over the likes of Yoel Romero, Johnny Hendricks, Uriah Hall, Luke Rockhold and many more. He last fought at UFC 278 when he defeated Rockhold in a blood fest via unanimous decision. Costa has come short only twice in his MMA career so far against former middleweight title challenger Marvin Vettori and current champion Israel Adesanya.

    Costa is highly respected by the MMA community more for his meme game than his fighting style. Costa has a lot of gigs going on Twitter, one of them is trolling fighters here and there such as Adesanya since they’re both rivals and also trolls his friend, Paddy Pimblett. Costa can also use his troll energy to have an upper hand in serious back-and-forths. Costa’s most famous thing going on is “Secret Juice” which is a liquid that Costa ingests and makes people go suspicious about the question if he is escaping from the hands of the US-Anti Doping Agency or not, which ends up being a hilarious show on the internet for the meme makers.
