    Jenna Renee Webb is one of the hottest fitness and swimwear stars of our time. Her desire to eat the right things and have the perfect body started after suffering from an eating disorder as a teenager. After surviving the terrible disease, Webb decided to eat better to stay healthy and at the same time become an advocate for healthy eating as well as general fitness.

    As a model and fitness advocate, Jenna has worked with several fitness companies like Schiek Sports and MuscleTech and has graced the pages of several well-known magazines like Muscle & Fitness, Maxim, Sports Illustrated, Shape, Muscle Evolution, Planet Muscle, Oxygen Magazine, Musclemag, X-Fitness, Ironman, and many more. She was once married to UFC fighter Travis Browne. The couple tied the knot in early 2015 and similarly split in June after the model accused the fighter of domestic abuse. Their divorce was finalized in 2016.

    Who is Jenna Renee Webb? – His bio

    Jenna was born in Ormond Beach, Florida on September 9, 1987. Her journey to becoming one of America’s hottest models with the hottest body hasn’t been smooth sailing. She revealed that she went from 110lbs to 135lbs as a 10th grader which made her hate her body and feel terribly insecure.

    She developed an eating disorder which landed her in hospital and after surviving the disorder she was determined never to be a victim of another disorder so she started reading books and learning about the healthy eating habits. It was during this time that she stumbled upon the six meals a day program.

    Webb remembers being terrified of the program at first, but after trying it for a few weeks with constant exercise, she began to notice a huge improvement, so she kept going and the result of her hard work and dedication determination paid off. Today she has graced the pages of several well-known notable magazines like Muscle & Fitness, Maxim, Sports Illustrated, Shape, Muscle Evolution, Planet Muscle, Oxygen Magazine, Musclemag, X-Fitness, Ironman, and many more.

    Wondering what his diet looks like? Jenna Renee Webb told simplyshredded.com that she eats clean every day, all year round, except on special days like holidays or her birthday. Below is an example of his daily food schedule:

    • Meal 1: water, 5 egg whites, 1/2 cup rolled oats, 1 or 2 cups coffee
    • Meal 2: water, protein shake
    • Meal 3: grilled chicken breast, green beans
    • Meal 4: Water, Protein Shake with an Apple and Peanut Butter, or Homemade Protein Bar
    • Meal 5: Buffalo steak, 1/2 sweet potato, green beans
    • Meal 6: 6 egg whites and fat-free cottage cheese (the 6th meal is taken only when needed).

    And for supplements, she takes multivitamins, a pre-workout supplement (when she feels she needs them) of BCAAs, glutamine, and whey protein.

    His family

    Jenna is not the only child of her parents, she has two older siblings – a brother and a sister. The model was married to UFC fighter Travis Browne. The couple married in January 2015 and separated in June of that year after the model accused the fighter of domestic violence.

    Although Browne denies ever getting physical with Jenna, the model had photos to back up her claims. This resulted in the fighter being banned by the UFC until they complete their investigations and clear him of all allegations. Their divorce was finalized in 2016.

    Travis has since moved on with his life. He is currently dating former women’s bantamweight champion Ronda Rousey, whom he reportedly saw while she was still married to Jenna.

    Other Facts About Jenna Renee Webb

    If Jenna chose three exercises, it would be squats, pull-ups, and lunges. One food she couldn’t live without was eggs.

    Her greatest motivation is to see the results of her hard work. Another thing that she says has really helped her stay motivated is taking pictures of herself.

    While other women may be turned on by a man’s abs or whatever you’ve got, Webb finds a man’s neck and shoulders to be most appealing to her, she considers those the parts. the sexiest parts of a man’s body.

    The workout routine that has worked best for her involves training one muscle group per week with a two-day recovery, then doing less cardio and more weights.
