    Kevin Love’s wife, Kate Bock, is a successful international swimsuit model who has graced the pages of numerous magazines and worked with many high-profile brands.

    Kate Bock rose to fame during her time with Sports Illustrated. 

    She and Kevin had been together for nearly six years before the NBA star proposed to her on her 33rd birthday in January 2021. Bock shared the news of their engagement on Instagram.

    Their glamorous Great Gatsby-themed wedding took place at the New York Public Library in late June 2022, with several NBA stars in attendance, including LeBron James, Russell Westbrook, Darius Garland, and Jordan Clarkson.

    Bock took to Instagram to express her joy about the event, writing, “It was everything. The ceremony was perfect, the venue was stunning, the guests were all smiles, and we couldn’t have felt more loved. We only wish we could have savored every moment for longer.”

    Who is Kevin Love’s wife, Kate?

    Kate Bock is a well-known model who has appeared in numerous magazines throughout her career. 

    She has been featured in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue since 2013, and in 2020, she shared the cover with Olivia Culpo and Jasmine Sanders. 

    Bock has also graced the cover of Maxim magazine and has been featured in various issues of Elle. In addition to her magazine appearances, Bock has worked with Victoria’s Secret as one of their models. 

    She was discovered at the age of 12 in Vancouver, Canada, and later moved to Paris after finishing high school, where she became fluent in French.

    Bock has also modeled for other brands, such as Guess, Ralph Lauren, and Vogue. She has a significant following on Instagram, where she goes by @katelove and has 946k followers.

    Is Kevin Love still associated with the Cleveland Cavaliers?

    As of February 16, 2023, Kevin Love is still a Cleveland Cavaliers NBA team member. However, rumors have been circulating about a potential departure from the team. 

    Kevin Love’s time with the Cleveland Cavaliers seems to end, as the team and Love are reportedly finalizing a contract buyout that would make him a free agent. 

    Sources suggest that the Miami Heat are likely to show interest in the five-time All-Star once he becomes a free agent.

    If the buyout is completed, Love will have until March 1 to sign with another team and be eligible for the playoffs.

    Love was a significant part of Cleveland’s championship team in 2016 and is the last remaining major player from that season still on the roster.

    Despite his successful track record, Love has not been a part of the Cavs’ rotation in the past 12 games, which has led to speculation about his future with the team. 

    Reports suggest that Love’s management has requested a buyout of the remainder of his 4-year, $120 million contract.
