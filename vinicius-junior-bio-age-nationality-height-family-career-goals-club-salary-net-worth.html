    Vinícius José Paixão de Oliveira Júnior, popularly known as Vini Jr or Vinícius Júnior.
    Vini Jr started his professional career at Flamengo in 2017. He spent 2 years with Flamengo but his performance was enough to earn him a move to Real Madrid Castilla.

    Vinícius though came to Real Madrid Castilla in 2018-2019, he made some appearances for the Real Madrid senior team in that very year and became a full-time member in 2019-2020. He has since then made 191 appearances for Real Madrid and scored 47 goals so far.

    Most scouts knew about Vini Jr back at Flamengo but he earned his current reputation and recognition due to his incredible performances at Real Madrid.
