    Wiki/Biography

    Career

    Maya Ali started her career as a journalist with a newspaper and advanced to VJ with Samaa TV and went on to work at Waqt News and Dunya News.

    Maya Ali Anchoring

    She made her acting debut with the Pakistani TV serial “Durr-e-Shahwar” (2012) in which, she played the role of ‘Mahnoor Sami.’

    Maya Ali in a Scene from ‘Durr-e-Shahwar’

    Maya’s first serial in the lead role was with Geo TV’s “Aik Nayee Cinderella” (2012-2013) in which, she was seen as ‘Meesha/Cinderella.’

    She did many hit Pakistani serials such as Aunn Zara (2013), Khoya Khoya Chand (2013), Shanakht (2014), Zid (2014–2015), Mera Naam Yousuf Hai (2015), Mann Mayal (2016), and Sanam (2016-2017). She made her film debut as an actor with the 2018 Pakistani film “Teefa in Trouble” as ‘Anya.’ The film marked the Pakistani film debut of Ali Zafar and was a huge commercial in Pakistan. It is also the first non-Indian film, which was distributed internationally by Yash Raj Films.

    She then appeared in the Pakistani film “Parey Hut Love” (2019) as ‘Saniya.’ Reportedly, Maya was slated to make her Bollywood debut with Akshay Kumar‘s film “Ikka,” which is a remake of the Tamil film “Kaththi” (2014).

    Facts/Trivia

    • Maya Ali likes reading books, watching films and shows, and travelling.
    • She is more popular with her pseudonym ‘Maya.’

In an interview, she revealed that she wanted to make her career in sports but due to the lack of family support in her life, she had to quit her dream.

About six months prior to her father’s death, their relation improved.
