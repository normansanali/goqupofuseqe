    In an age when new skincare pops onto shelves at seemingly unprecedented speeds, there’s much to be said for a brand that’s remained highly relevant since 1840. Palmer’s, established over 180 years ago, is one such example. Known for its anti-aging bodycare hits, including a firming butter shoppers call their “secret to no wrinkles,” Palmer’s is also the purveyor of a highly popular, surprisingly affordable anti-aging face oil.  

    The oil is a decided favorite among Amazon shoppers — over 29,000 of whom have given it a perfect five-star rating on the site. Yet another testament to its excellence: The product is the number one best-seller in Amazon’s densely populated facial oil category. And, for a limited time, Palmer’s highly lauded elixir is on sale for a mere $10. 

    Palmer’s Skin Therapy Face Oil is a highly skin-softening and lightweight skin treatment. Formulated for all skin types, it features a cocktail of anti-aging vitamins plus retinol — the line-smoothing and skin-firming hero ingredient that’s considered, virtually indisputably, skincare’s preeminent anti-ager. Key benefits include fading dark spots, minimizing the appearance of lines and wrinkles, and improving uneven skin tone. Though it’s technically an oil, the formula features a velvety, lightweight feel and a fast-absorbing texture. A single use delivers an instant boost in glow, per shoppers; over time, skin appears firmer, smoother, and more even-toned. 

    Each of the oil’s key ingredients — vitamin C, vitamin E, and retinol — boast ample dermatologist acclaim. Moreover, vitamin C and E have been found to work remarkably well together insofar as their age-defying properties, as they protect one another from degradation and, in turn, boost effectiveness. Vitamin C is a skin-brightening antioxidant that can even tone and has protective effects against environmental skin-agers, while vitamin E, another antioxidant, is also hailed for its supple-inducing properties. 

    One shopper called the face oil “magic,” noting that their skin looks glowier than ever since using it. Another reviewer who deals with notable facial redness said the formula has reduced much of the issue. Plus, they added that the oil “leaves [their] skin glowy but not greasy.” Another reviewer said it makes their skin “so soft and smooth,” as well as “supple.”  Finally, another fan “over 30” called the oil “rich [and] hydrating” and said that it “smells wonderful” to boot. They noted that it “works like a luxurious $125 oil” they used to use and that “it has totally transformed the appearance of [their] skin,” reducing “wrinkles, brown spots, and redness.”

    For a glow-inducing, lightweight oil, shop Amazon’s best selling Palmer’s Skin Therapy Oil while it’s $10 for a limited time.
