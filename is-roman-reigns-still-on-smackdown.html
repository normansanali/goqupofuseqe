    WrestleMania 40 Highlights

    At WrestleMania 40, Roman Reigns was involved in a highly anticipated storyline. He, alongside The Rock, faced Cody Rhodes and Seth Rollins in a tag team match that headlined Night 1 of the event.

    The outcome of this match had major implications for Night 2, especially regarding Reigns' potential match against Cody Rhodes for the undisputed WWE Universal Championship.
