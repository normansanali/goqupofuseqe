    How did Shawn Soares die? What was his Cause of death?

    Tragedy struck on the Virginia Commonwealth University (VCU) campus as a collision between two vehicles resulted in the death of a student, Shawn Soares. The incident was reported to the Richmond Police Department at around 6:45 p.m. on Thursday, May 5, after a collision with personal injury was witnessed on the 300 block of West Main Street. According to the police, the two vehicles involved were heading westbound on West Main Street, traveling in the same direction. One of the vehicles left the roadway and collided with a pole and an adult male pedestrian, before crashing into a nearby structure. Unfortunately, Shawn Soares, the pedestrian who was struck, was found severely injured at the scene and later passed away due to his injuries.

    According to his social media profile, he was pursuing a Master of Science in Business Administration degree. This tragic incident is a reminder of the importance of road safety and the need for all individuals to exercise caution while on the road, whether as drivers or pedestrians.

    After the collision on West Main Street, the area around Madison Street was closed for several hours so that crews could fix the damaged pole. The Richmond Police Department’s Crash Team is still investigating the incident, and both vehicles involved in the accident have been left in place.
