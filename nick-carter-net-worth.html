    Nick Carter Wiki Biography

    Nickolas Gene Carter, simply known as Nick Carter, is a famous American singer and songwriter, record producer, musician, as well as an actor. To the public, Nick Carter is perhaps best known for his involvement with the popular vocal group called “Backstreet Boys”. Formed in 1993, the band also included AJ McLean, Howie Dorough, Kevin Richardson and Brian Littrell. The “Backstreet Boys” rose to prominence in 1996 with the release of their self-titled studio album, which brought them a lot of international attention. In addition to producing five singles, the album managed to top the music charts in Austria, Canada, Germany and Malaysia. The group followed their successful debut with “Backstreet’s Back”, and “Backstreet Boys”, both of which came out in 1997. The latter self-titled album was released solely in the United States, where it enjoyed large commercial success. “Backstreet Boys” attracted public attention with such singles as “Everybody (Backstreet’s Back)”, “As Long As You Love Me”, and “All I Have to Give”, all of which contributed to the sales of the album. Up to date, the album has sold approximately 14 million copies, and earned a 14-times Platinum certification. Since their debut, both internationally and in the United States, “Backstreet Boys” have grown to become one of the best-selling groups in music history, with more than 130 million albums sold around the world. More recently, in 2013, in order to celebrate their 20th anniversary the band released their eighth studio album called “In a World Like This”.
