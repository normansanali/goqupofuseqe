    How many losses does Ryan Garcia have? – Ryan Garcia is an accomplished American professional boxer, born on August 8, 1998.

    He first came into the spotlight in 2017, winning his first 13 fights via knockout. In 2021, Garcia held the WBC interim lightweight title, adding to his already impressive record.

    As of May 2022, Garcia is ranked as the world’s sixth-best active lightweight by several notable boxing publications, including The Ring magazine, the Transnational Boxing Rankings Board, and ESPN. BoxRec ranks him as the tenth-best active lightweight.

    Ryan Garcia’s success in the ring is due in part to the support of his family. His parents played an active role in his amateur career and continue to assist him with his professional career. His father serves as one of his trainers, while his mother works as his Personal Administrative Assistant for commercial projects. In March 2019, Garcia became a father himself.

    While Ryan Garcia is American, he proudly incorporates his Mexican heritage into his persona. This is evident in his choice of attire, which often includes both the American and Mexican flags and the colors of red, white, and blue. From October 2018 to February 2022, Garcia was trained by Eddy Reynoso, who also trains boxing greats Canelo Álvarez and Óscar Valdez at their gym in San Diego, California. He is currently trained by Joe Goosen.

    In December 2022, Garcia began training in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu under Rener Gracie. This move shows his commitment to constantly improving and expanding his skillset as a fighter.

    Garcia’s success in the ring has also led to endorsement deals outside of boxing. In 2021, he became the first American boxer to appear in a Gatorade commercial, joining NBA star Damian Lillard in a television ad campaign for the sports drink.

    With his talent, drive, and strong support system, Ryan Garcia is a force to be reckoned with in the boxing world, and fans eagerly await his next moves both in and out of the ring.

    How many losses does Ryan Garcia have?

    Ryan Garcia has been involved in 24 fights. He has been able to win 23 of those fights with his only defeat coming against Tank Davis on April 23, 2023. Out of his 23 wins, 19 have been technical knockouts.
