    Meet Michael Cardwell, Anna Chickadee Cardwell Husband

    Michael Cardwell and Anna Chickadee Cardwell are no longer together. However, the estranged exes reportedly exchanged their wedding vows in May 2014 after getting engaged on 19 December 2013. However, they ended up separating in April 2017. In a Facebook post at the time, Anna declared the former couple’s breakup.

    “For those who haven’t [sic] seen mine and Michael’s status we are separated it’s just a lot on both of us and have a lot to think about but this is only temporary right now I love him and always will no matter where this goes so people I ask don’t be blowing up my inbox [please] and thank [you],” Anna proclaimed on her post.

    The exes reportedly shared one child together named Kylee Madison who they welcomed in 2015. Meanwhile, Anna has another daughter named Kaitlyn Elizabeth born in 2012 from her former relationship.

    Michael has also moved on and is currently engaged to his fiance London Smith. On 12 March 2022, on his birthday, London wished her boyfriend, “Happy birthday to my person and my best friend! So thankful I get to be a part of your life and help you celebrate your birthday. Thank you for simply being you. You’ve brought so much joy back into my life. I love you so much. Have fun fishing today. I can’t wait to see you.”

    They have been in a relationship since 20 December 2021.

    Meanwhile, starting in 2017, Anna has moved on and is currently dating Eldridge Toney. Although having been together for a while, Anna said she is not in a hurry to be married because of her past marriage to Michael.

    “You never know what will happen,” Anna opened up to The Sun in June 2021, adding that she and her man wear promise rings. “I always tell him marriage is just a piece of paper saying, ‘Hey you’re married.’ I told myself if we make it past four years, then we can talk more about it.”

    Furthermore, in the exclusive interview, Anna shared about the tragic four miscarriages she had to go through with Eldridge.

    Anna said: “We are trying to have a third child, and it’s been difficult. I’ve gone through four miscarriages and a D&C. It’s been a long bumpy road for us trying to have baby number three.”

    She added, “The last miscarriage was two days before Christmas, and I had to get a D&C done. It felt like I was giving birth, it was the worst pain of my life. It’s a really painful process, so I’m like you know let’s give it one more try.”

    They’ve identified one potential root cause, the Honey Boo Boo star continued. Anna revealed, “I don’t make enough progesterone. I don’t have enough in my system, so it keeps the baby from getting enough nutrition.”

    According to Anna and Eldridge, they have not yet given up hope of having a rainbow baby. She said: “We know what’s going on now, and we are taking the right precautions to take care of it and make sure it stays alive and everything like that.”
